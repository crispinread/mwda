Web Developer Apprenticeship

Created by DEVELOPERS

A government funded technical apprenticeship scheme for web development. 12 month programme of industry led, learner focussed training and mentored on-the-job experience.

"ALL our trainers are practicing web development profesionals and experts in their field with minimum 10 years experience."

How it works

  1. Selection - we find candidates with the correct aptitude and enthusiasm
  2. Bootcamp - we ready these applicants for entering the workplace
  3. Placement - We train the applicants and support your team in getting the best from them

Costs and Funding

Through government funding our excellent training is available at no cost to your business * 

We also offer free consultancy and support to businesses and teams to make sure you get the most out of hiring an apprentice.

As the employer, you would pay the apprentice's salary, minimum by law is just £3.30 per hour but all employers we work with are paying at least £5 ph ~ £9K pa

* The training itself is match funded by the government and grants are available to employers to cover costs so that, in the vast majority of cases, it is cost neutral.
